2020: Europe's COVID-19 Spending Spree Unmasked

A good part of 2020, I worked with the OCCRP on collecting and analysing European COVID-19 public tender data. We have collected 21 billion euros worth of tender data from 37 countries. Article: https://www.occrp.org/en/coronavirus/europes-covid-19-spending-spree-unmasked OpenContracting blog: https://www.open-contracting.org/2021/02/04/unmasking-emergency-procurement-how-we-published-37000-european-covid-19-related-tenders/ Github repository: https://github.com/occrp/COVID-19-spending-2020

2020: Lecturer at masters in Investigative Journalism

In the autumn 2020 I was part of the team teaching data skills to master students at the University of Gothenburg. Website: https://www.gu.se/en/study-gothenburg/masters-programme-in-investigative-journalism-s2jou
